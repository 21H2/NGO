Frequently Asked Questions

What is Ek Nayi Soch?

Ek Nayi Soch is a non-profit organization based in Noida, India. We are dedicated to empowering underprivileged communities through education, healthcare, and skill development initiatives.

How can I donate to Ek Nayi Soch?

You can donate to Ek Nayi Soch through our website by visiting the 'Donate' page. We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, and UPI.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, donations made to Ek Nayi Soch are eligible for tax deduction under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961. We provide a receipt for your donation which can be used for tax purposes.

How can I volunteer with Ek Nayi Soch?

We welcome volunteers who want to contribute their time and skills. Please contact us through our website or email us at volunteer@eknayisochnoida.com for current volunteering opportunities and how to apply.

What percentage of my donation goes directly to programs?

At Ek Nayi Soch, we strive to ensure that the maximum amount of your donation goes directly to our programs. Approximately 80-85% of donations are used for program implementation, while the remaining is used for administrative and fundraising costs.
